About Dataset

For the project I decided to work on a cleaned and consolidated dataset created from the BRFSS 2015 dataset available on Kaggle as “Diabetes Health Indictor BRFSS2015 dataset”. The dataset contains 70,692 survey responses and has an equal 50-50 split of respondents with no diabetes and with either prediabetes or diabetes. It is a. The target variable has two classes: 0 is for no diabetes 1 is for prediabetes or diabetes. In total the dataset has 22 feature variables including diabetes variable.

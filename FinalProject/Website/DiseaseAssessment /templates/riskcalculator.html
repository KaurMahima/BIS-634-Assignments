Calculate Your Diabetes Risk


DIARISK is a prediction tool to identify patients at risk of developing diabetes. It requires no laboratory testing and has been validated in multiple populations. DIARISK uses age, BMI, physical health,general health, mental health, prevalence of blood Pressure, education level, income level, and gender to determine risk of developing diabetes. By using DIARISK to identify high-risk people and applying an educational intervention, it has been shown possible to reduce the incidence of diabetes.

Summary Statistics Insights:

  • The Dataset had 70692 rows and 22 columns.
  • There are no missing values in the dataset.
  • There are 2181 outliers in BMI.
  • There are 11816 outliers in Mental Health.
  • There are 10624 outliers in Physical Health.
  • High blood pressure count and high cholesterol count was more among the diabetic patients.
  • Interestingly cholesterol check in the past 5year had higher count than among the non-diabetic group.
  • The mean BMI is 29.5 kg/m2. The minimum and maximum BMI was 12 kg/m2 and 98kg/m2 respectively.
